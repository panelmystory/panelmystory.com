Questions

FREQUENTLY ASKED

WHAT DO I NEED TO SUBMIT?

Just 3–10 photos and a quick description of the key moments you want captured. We handle everything else from story to panels.

HOW LONG DOES IT TAKE?

Standard delivery is 7–10 days. Rush delivery (3 days) is available for an additional $25 at checkout.

WHAT DO I RECEIVE?

A high-resolution print-ready PDF at 300 DPI — suitable for professional printing up to 18×24 inches, or beautiful on any screen.

CAN I REQUEST REVISIONS?

Every order includes one round of minor revisions — text changes, panel swaps, or small adjustments. Additional revisions available.

WHAT THEMES DO YOU COVER?

Parents & family life, travel adventures, pets, birthdays, anniversaries, pregnancy announcements — if it's a story, we can panel it.

CAN I GET IT PRINTED?

We deliver digital files optimized for printing. We recommend services like Artifact Uprising, Printful, or your local print shop.
